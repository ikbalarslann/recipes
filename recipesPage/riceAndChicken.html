Rice and Chicken

Description

A most popular dish with chicken: Chicken Rice! I’m going to show how we make. Chicken Kapama “Tavuk Kapama”

Ingrediens

  • 1 whole chicken except breast part or about 8-10 pieces of drumstick
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 2+1/2 cup long grain rice like Turkish “Osmancık” or “Baldo” or “Cal Rose“
  • About 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. To soak the rice; add rice to a large bowl, then add warm water and salt. Wait at least for half an hour or up to 5 hours.
  2. Dice onions and saute in a wide pan in olive oil.
  3. Over the sautéed onions, add your soaked rice after draining well, and wash a couple of times to get rid of the starch. Continue to sauté the rice couple of more minutes. Don’t add salt too much because we used salt for the chicken stock as well.
  4. Level the sautéed rice and place the chicken pieces on top of it.
  5. Add 3+1/2 cups chicken stock, cover the lid. Bake in the preheated 170C degree oven until rice is soft. Check after about 25 minutes. After the rice is soft open the lid and broil until the chicken skin has light golden color.
